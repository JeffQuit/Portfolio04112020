About: Jeff Quittman

Jeff Quittman is an emerging full stack software developer with a diverse background in Graphic Design, 3D CAD Modeling, Video-game Level Design, Photography, and much much more. Jeff is currently the Director of Program Success at Tuition.io, the innovator and industry leader in the student loan repayment employee benefit. As a natural problem solver and creator, Jeff approaches each problem with curiosity and multiple problem solving techniques that allow him to resolve any issue or overcome any obstacle in his path. In his free time, Jeff is an avid paintball player where he maintains a collection of vintage paintball markers. As a part-time job, he works at Ambush Paintball Park in Moorpark, CA.
